Publish Rides

Offering a carpool/bikepool ride on RideSharely is easy. To publish your ride, use our RideSharely.com. Indicate your departure and arrival points, the date and time of your departure, how many passengers you can take and the price per seat. You’ll also need to choose how you want to accept bookings , and you have the option of adding any important details you think your passengers should know about. Then tap ‘Publish ride’ and you’re done!

On RideSharely

Carpooling/Bikepooling with RideSharely is super easy, and free! Simply sign up for an account and tell us some basic details about yourself. Once you have a RideSharely account, you can start booking or publishing rides directly on our website.
