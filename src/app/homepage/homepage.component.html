FAQs

How do I book a carpool/bikepool ride ?

You can book a vehiclepool ride on our ridesharely.com. Simply search for your destination, choose the date you want to travel and pick the carpool or bikepool that suits you best! Rides require manual approval from the driver. Either way, booking a carpool ride is fast, simple and easy.

How do I cancel my carpool/bikepool ride ?

If you have a change of plans, you can always cancel your carpool ride from the ‘Your rides’ section of our app. The sooner you cancel, the better. That way the driver has time to accept new passengers.

How much does a carpool ride cost?

The costs of a carpool ride can vary greatly, and depend on factors like distance, the demand of that ride and more . We charge 5rs per km . Check out some of our top carpool destinations to get an idea of price, or start searching for your next carpool/bikepool ride on RideSharely.com.

How do I publish a carpool/bikepool ride?

Offering a carpool/bikepool ride on RideSharely is easy. To publish your ride, use our RideSharely.com. Indicate your departure and arrival points, the date and time of your departure, how many passengers you can take and the price per seat. You’ll also need to choose how you want to accept bookings , and you have the option of adding any important details you think your passengers should know about. Then tap ‘Publish ride’ and you’re done!

What are the benefits of travelling by carpool/bikepool ?

There are multiple advantages to carpooling, over other means of transport. Travelling by carpool is usually more affordable, especially for longer distances. Carpooling/bikepool is also more eco-friendly, as sharing a car/bike means there will be fewer cars and bike on the road, and therefore fewer emissions. Taking a carpool/bikepool ride is also a safe way to travel in the current times. Because there are only a few people in a car/bike, you have fewer points of contact and there’s less risk than other travel options.

How do I start carpooling/bikepooling ?

Carpooling/Bikepooling with RideSharely is super easy, and free! Simply sign up for an account and tell us some basic details about yourself. Once you have a RideSharely account, you can start booking or publishing rides directly on our website.

How is RideSharely different than other carpooling cab services?

RideSharely is not a cab service. It is an online carpool as well as bikepool platform that connects Driver(s) and Rider(s). RideSharely caters exclusively to working professionals, who are verified riders.

How secure is the platform ?

RideSharely is only open to professionals and we verify users through their email and mobile. We ensure that all Users have their privacy protected by routing all calls through our website .

How is the verification done ?

We send an email verification to your email ID that you provide us at the time of creation of your profile. Click on the link given in the mail which will automatically verify the account. Or if we talk about contact than we will send you OTP to your number that you provide us at the time of creation of your profile.
