How do I cancel my carpool ride?

If you have a change of plans, you can always cancel your carpool ride from the ‘Your rides’ section of our app.

The sooner you cancel, the better.

That way the driver has time to accept new passengers. The amount of your refund will depend on how far in advance you cancel.

If you cancel more than 24 hours before departure, for example, you’ll receive a full refund, excluding the service fee.
