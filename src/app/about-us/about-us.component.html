About RideSharely

RideSharely is India’s largest online Car and Bike riding platform that has transformed Car and Bike travel in the city by bringing ease and convenience to thousands of people who travel daily. Founded in 2022, RideSharely is part of India’s leading online travel company MakeMyTrip Limited (NASDAQ: MMYT). By providing widest choice, superior customer service, lowest prices and unmatched benefits, RideSharely has served over thousands customers.
